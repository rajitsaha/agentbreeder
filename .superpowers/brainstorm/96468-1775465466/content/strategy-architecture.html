Which strategic architecture fits your goals?

Three ways to combine personal thought leadership + AgentBreeder product launch. These are fundamentally different plays.

A

Product-First — "Build it and they will come"

Lead with AgentBreeder. Personal brand follows product traction.

→ Focus: GitHub stars, developer adoption, Show HN
→ You become known as "the person who built AgentBreeder"
→ Thesis emerges from product, not the other way around
✗ Slow to build executive/VC credibility. Doesn't differentiate your personal voice.
B

Thought Leadership-First — "The Idea Precedes the Product"

Lead with the thesis. AgentBreeder is proof, not the pitch.

→ Publish the big idea first: essays, LinkedIn, keynotes, podcasts
→ Build an audience who believes the thesis before they see the product
→ VCs/CTOs/CEOs follow ideas, not products — this is how you reach them
~ Requires consistent content output. Product launch can feel delayed.
C

⭐ Category Creation — "Name the Problem, Own the Solution"

Define a new category nobody has named yet. Become the person who coined it. AgentBreeder is the reference implementation.

→ The Category: "Agent Revenue Operations" — AI agents as the direct path from data/ML to business outcomes
→ You publish the manifesto. VCs fund category creators, not feature builders.
→ AgentBreeder = the platform that makes this category real and operational
→ Open source community validates the thesis. Enterprise pays to operationalize it.
This is how Hashicorp (DevOps infrastructure), Datadog (cloud observability), and dbt (data transformation) won. They named the problem before they built the solution.
