# Eval Criteria — microlearning-ebook-agent
Generated by /agent-build. Business goal: "Help corporate L&D teams produce bite-sized training material on demand without hiring instructional designers."

## Eval Dimensions

### Citation Accuracy
**Pass:** Every factual claim in the ebook is traceable to a source returned by `research_web`. URLs resolve. Source publication date is included.
**Fail:** Any claim without a citation. Citation points to a URL that 404s or to a source the agent never actually fetched.

### Hallucination Rate
**Pass:** Zero invented statistics, dates, names, or quotes. When sources disagree, the ebook surfaces the disagreement instead of fabricating consensus.
**Fail:** Any number, named entity, or quoted text that does not appear in the cited sources.

### Content Completeness
**Pass:** Output contains intro, 3-6 lesson modules, a quiz per lesson, capstone summary, and go-deeper reading list. Total reading time 20-40 min.
**Fail:** Missing any structural section. Reading time outside the 20-40 min target band.

### Pedagogical Structure Quality
**Pass:** Lessons progress through Bloom's taxonomy (remember -> understand -> apply). Each lesson states its learning outcome up front and recaps it at the end.
**Fail:** Lessons jump straight to application without foundational concepts. Learning outcomes missing or vague.

### Quiz Answer Correctness
**Pass:** Each quiz question has exactly one correct answer that is verifiable from the lesson body. Distractors are plausible but unambiguously wrong. Explanations cite the lesson section.
**Fail:** Multiple correct answers. Trivially obvious distractors ("none of the above" filler). Explanations contradict the lesson.

## Starter Test Cases

| Input | Expected behavior | Eval dimension |
|---|---|---|
| "Create a microlearning ebook on Zero Trust networking for non-technical managers." | 3-5 lessons covering identity, segmentation, monitoring; every claim cited; quizzes test understanding not memorization | Citation accuracy, Pedagogical structure |
| "Build a microlearning module on SOC 2 controls for new engineers." | Lessons grouped by trust service criteria; cites AICPA + practitioner sources; quizzes have plausible distractors based on common misconceptions | Citation accuracy, Quiz correctness |
| "Topic: Negotiation tactics for first-time managers." | Lessons follow Bloom's taxonomy: define BATNA -> recognize tactics -> apply in role-play scenario; reading time ~30 min | Pedagogical structure, Content completeness |
| "Generate an ebook on the GDPR right to erasure for our customer support team." | Surfaces sources of legal disagreement (e.g., backups vs deletion timing) rather than picking one; no PII in examples | Hallucination rate, Citation accuracy |
