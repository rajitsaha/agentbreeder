# .antigravity.md — What NOT To Do With microlearning-ebook-agent

## Model
- Do NOT swap to a cheaper model without re-running `tests/evals/`
- Do NOT change `model.primary` in `agent.yaml` without updating `.cursorrules`
- Do NOT lower temperature below 0.2 — quizzes need varied distractors

## Memory
- Do NOT store learner PII in PostgreSQL without a retention policy
- Do NOT let HITL-pending sessions accumulate forever — reap after 7 days
- Do NOT cache web research results past 30 days — sources go stale

## Tools
- Do NOT add tools without tests in `tests/`
- Do NOT call external APIs directly in `agent.py` — always via `tools/`
- Do NOT expose internal errors or stack traces via tool outputs
- Do NOT short-circuit research_web with a static knowledge base — that defeats "researches it"

## Deploy
- Do NOT deploy without `agentbreeder validate` passing
- Do NOT hardcode region, account IDs, or project names — use env vars
- Do NOT skip the health check endpoint — Cloud Run needs it for traffic routing
- Do NOT raise min instances above 0 — scale-to-zero is the cost story for low-volume L&D use

## Evals
- Do NOT merge changes that drop any eval dimension below its baseline
- Do NOT add eval test cases that always pass — they provide no signal
- Do NOT remove the citation-accuracy eval — it is the firewall against hallucination

## Content
- Do NOT generate content the agent cannot cite
- Do NOT pick a side when sources disagree — surface the disagreement to the user
- Do NOT skip the HITL outline approval step — it is the L&D reviewer's quality gate
